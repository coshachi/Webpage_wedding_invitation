How to get there:

Marcin and I are looking forward to celebrating our wedding with you. Hopefully, the information bellow will help in planning your journey to our homeland.

Ours is a two-day wedding Saturday (7th) and Sunday (8th) of August 2021 in a small town called Świlcza, near Rzeszów.

There are several options to get there.

Rzeszów
Rzeszów has an airport to which you can fly directly from Dublin with Ryanair. The flights to and from Rzeszów operate three times a week (Tuesday, Thursday and Sunday). Świlcza is only 16km away from Rzeszów airport. You can get there by taxi (about €13-15) or you can rent a car at the airport.
Kraków
Alternatively, you can fly to Kraków (any day). Then hop on the train from Kraków Central and get to Rzeszów Central and again get a taxi to Świlcza which is again only 10km away (cost should be €13-15 as well).

You find all available trains and buses here: https://en.e-podroznik.pl/ (more options will become available closer to the time).

To find best flight options check skyscanner: https://www.skyscanner.ie/ (more options will become available closer to the time).
